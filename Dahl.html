Red Lentil Dahl

This red lentil dahl is is a must try if your're looking for a flavorful curry recipe. The red lentils are healthy AND tasty and will warm you up in

the cold weather.

Ingredients

For the dahl

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 large yellow onion, chopped small
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, peeled and grated
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper chili flakes
  • 1 1/2 cups dried red lentils
  • 14 ounce can diced tomatoes
  • 13.5 ounce can full fat coconut milk
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • half a lemon, juiced
  • 3-4 cups baby spinach

    • For serving

  • 4 cups cooked brown or white rice
  • Vegan Naan

Preparation

  1. In a large pot or pan over medium heat, saut√© the chopped onion in the olive oil for 5 minutes, stirring frequently.
  2. Then add the garlic and ginger and cook 1 more minute, until fragrant.
  3. Add the garam masala, turmeric and red pepper flakes to the pan and stir into the onion mixture.
  4. Add a few tablespoons of water if the mixture is too dry.
  5. Now add the dried lentils, canned tomatoes and their juices, coconut milk and vegetable broth to the pan. Stir well and turn the heat to high.
  6. Bring to a boil, then lower heat and simmer for about 15 minutes, until the lentils are cooked and soft. Stir occasionally.
  7. Squeeze the lemon juice into the pan, and stir in the spinach as well until wilted. Add salt to taste. I used 1 teaspoon.
  8. Serve with brown or white rice and Vegan Naan. Enjoy!
